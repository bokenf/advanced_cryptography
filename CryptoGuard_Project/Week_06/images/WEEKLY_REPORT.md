## 3. Student Reflection
This week, I transitioned from the Feistel structure studied in Week 5 to the SPN architecture used in modern standards like AES. Unlike Feistel networks that split data into halves, the SPN processes the entire block, utilizing S-Boxes for non-linear confusion and bit-permutations for diffusion.
