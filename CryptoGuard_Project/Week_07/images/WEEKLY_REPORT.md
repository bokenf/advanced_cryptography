## 3. Student Reflection
This week, I transitioned from designing cipher architectures to analyzing their security against cryptanalytic attacks. I explored both differential and linear cryptanalysis, simulating how attackers use XOR relations, input differences, and statistical frequency distributions to reveal vulnerabilities. By developing the Block Cipher Security Analyzer and documenting the resilience of AES, I gained a practical understanding of why modern encryption standards are effective against these specific algebraic and statistical threats.
